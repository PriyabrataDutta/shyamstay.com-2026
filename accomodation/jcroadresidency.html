About This Room

Located right off JC Road in central Bengaluru, Shyam Stay Pure Veg Boys PG – JC Road offers a comfortable, secure, and fully vegetarian PG stay tailored for male students and working professionals. With spacious, clean AC and non‑AC rooms, this PG is perfect for those attending nearby institutions like CMS Jain, Christ University, and St. Joseph’s.

Key Points To Consider

  • Walking distance from Christ University
  • Homely Veg Meals (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)
  • 24x7 Security & CCTV
  • Free Wi-Fi & Laundry
  • Comfortable Rooms with Attached Bath
  • Power Backup & Lift

House Rules

  • Entry strictly allowed till 9:30 PM unless prior permission is taken.
  • No outside guests or overnight stays allowed in the rooms.
  • Smoking, alcohol, and drugs are strictly prohibited on the premises.
  • Maintain cleanliness in rooms and common areas. Housekeeping is weekly.
  • Noise levels should be kept low after 10 PM to ensure a peaceful environment.
  • Any damage to PG property will be chargeable to the resident.
  • Residents must carry their ID card and show it when asked by staff/security.
